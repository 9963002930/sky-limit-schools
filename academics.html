Academic Programs

Sky Limit School offers a comprehensive and rigorous academic program designed to challenge and inspire students at all grade levels. Our curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning.

Subjects Offered

Mathematics

Our mathematics program focuses on developing problem-solving skills, logical reasoning, and a strong foundation in mathematical concepts. We offer courses from basic arithmetic to advanced calculus.

Science

Our science curriculum includes biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science. Students engage in hands-on experiments and research projects to explore the wonders of the natural world.

English Language Arts

Our English program emphasizes reading, writing, and communication skills. Students study classic and contemporary literature, develop their writing abilities, and engage in meaningful discussions.

Social Studies

Our social studies curriculum covers history, geography, civics, and economics. Students learn about different cultures, historical events, and the principles of democracy and citizenship.

Foreign Languages

We offer foreign language courses in Spanish, French, and Mandarin. Students gain proficiency in speaking, listening, reading, and writing, and learn about the cultures associated with these languages.

Arts and Music

Our arts program includes visual arts, music, drama, and dance. Students explore their creativity through various forms of artistic expression and performance.
